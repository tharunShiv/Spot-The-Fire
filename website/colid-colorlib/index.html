Frequently Asked Questions

Is internet connection necessary to use this App?

Since this application is Crowd Sourced, we need real time information from the people, hence Internet is necessary

What is the time taken to get help through the App?

It takes an average time of 40 seconds

How do you differentiate between different intensities of fire?

We have used Neural Networks to detect fire, the neurons in the neural network work in an abstract way but in an accurate way.

  1. One can inform fire fighters in just one click without even talking or typing anything.

  2. One can get the safe directions to his destination, without ending up being stuck at a fire disaster

  3. One can get instant emergency medical assistance on fire injuries.
